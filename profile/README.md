## Overview

Luminar Neo Advanced is an AI-powered photo editing application from Skylum that applies intelligent enhancement tools to landscape, portrait, and creative photography, producing dramatic results with minimal manual adjustment through the power of trained neural networks.

Sky AI detects and replaces any sky in a photograph with dramatic alternatives from a built-in library, automatically adjusting the lighting color, reflections, and atmospheric haze in the scene to match the replaced sky convincingly.

Portrait AI analyzes facial regions and applies targeted enhancements including skin smoothing, facial feature adjustments, eye brightening, and background separation, producing professional portrait retouching results without manual masking.

Atmosphere AI adds environmental effects including fog, haze, and light rays that integrate convincingly into the existing scene lighting, enabling creative atmospheric transformations that look photographic rather than digitally composited.

For photographers who want AI-powered creative tools that produce dramatic results quickly without extensive manual editing work, Luminar Neo Advanced is the photo editor that makes impressive transformations accessible through the power of trained AI models.

## Performance

Luminar Neo balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
